GitHub Classroom

Fixing critical onboarding issues uncovered during research — just in time for the beginning of the school semester

Overview

Professors and teachers sign up for GitHub Classroom to manage their students’ coding assignments. I had just joined the Classroom team when, in the process of conducting usability testing for a beta feature, I uncovered a more serious issue: many new users were left stranded during the signup flow (see simplified flow below) and disoriented about what to do next.

Through subsequent research sessions, we made improvements to the onboarding experience by revising the IA and setup sequence to align with teachers’ workflows and mental models. We shipped in time to see a nearly 30% increase in student assignments submitted in Q1.
