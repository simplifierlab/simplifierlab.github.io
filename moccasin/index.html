Project Moccasin

I launched a mentorship program to provide an opportunity for designers and PMs to get an insider perspective on how experienced product folks get their jobs done

Overview

Launched as a side project, Project Moccasin is a mini-mentorship program for PMs and designers where applicants experience what it's like to walk in someone's shoes by spending a day shadowing their mentor at their workplace. The program was over-subscribed — running twice in NYC and once in Austin — with additional interest from applicants in other cities.
