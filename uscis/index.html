USCIS

Applying human-centered design methods to reinvent the path to becoming a U.S. citizen

Overview

I led the “last mile” of the 18F team’s engagement with USCIS to improve citizenship services. By introducing lean principles and an experimentation mindset, and conducting more rigorous research instead of the casual “hallway testing” approach, we uncovered misalignment between the product direction and the market opportunity. Through design thinking workshops and ongoing design sprints, we were able to make course corrections to the product roadmap and deliver value to key target users.
