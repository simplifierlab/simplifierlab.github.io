USCIS

Applying human-centered design methods to reinvent the path to becoming a U.S. citizen

Overview

I led the “last mile” of the 18F team’s engagement with USCIS to improve citizenship services. I introduced lean principles and an experimentation mindset, facilitated design thinking workshops, and conducted rigorous, ongoing research so we could deliver product value to key target users.

A few artifacts shown below: (top) a photo taken during a user research session, (middle) a sample screen used for usability testing; (bottom) provisional personas derived from a compilation of user interviews.
