USCIS

On the heels of the much-improved relaunch of Healthcare.gov, a number of federal agencies wanted to follow suit and adopt a human-centered approach to designing public services. Accordingly, the United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) approached 18F to help them modernize immigration services. But with a mountain of pain points and unending opportunities for improvement, turns out, the trickiest challenge was aligning on priorities and pivoting on product strategy.
